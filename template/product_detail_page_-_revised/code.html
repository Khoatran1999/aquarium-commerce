About the Neon Tetra

The Neon Tetra (Paracheirodon innesi) is arguably the most popular freshwater tropical fish of all time. Native to the clearwater and blackwater streams in the Amazon basin of South America, these fish are celebrated for their dazzling colors and peaceful temperament.

They are shoaling fish and should be kept in groups of at least 6 to feel secure, though a larger school of 10 or more will result in much more natural behavior and better coloration. In a well-planted tank with subdued lighting, their iridescent blue and red stripes seem to glow, creating a spectacular display.
